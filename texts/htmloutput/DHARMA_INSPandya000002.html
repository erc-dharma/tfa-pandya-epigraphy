    Trichy, cave, time of Māṟañ Caṭaiyaṉ, year 4

    Metadata

    Current Version:  draft, 2024-06-06Z

    Editor:   Emmanuel Francis.

    DHARMA Identifier: INSPandya000002

    Summary: Endowment of gold for maintaining perpetual lamps.

    Hand Description:

    Tamil script. The puḷḷi is used, almost consistently.
